Data-driven Cybersecurity

Ten lessons built from Data-Driven Cybersecurity (Mariano Mattei, 2024), adapted for a security leader operationalising an audit-defensible metric programme at a 9-entity Vietnamese securities firm. Every lesson produces a Vietnamese artefact you can circulate to your CEO, board, audit committee, UBCK examiner, or team this week.

Arc 1 — Foundational trilogy

Lesson 0001 data-driven-cybersecurity 2026-07-19

Stop Counting Vulnerabilities: Start Measuring Program Health

Why the wrong question gets reported to the board. Mattei's "why vulnerability counts are the wrong metric" critique, the 10-row Vietnamese board pack for the CISO's first quarter, and the audit-defensible answer for "are we secure?" in 90 seconds.

Lesson 0002 data-driven-cybersecurity 2026-07-19

The METRICS Methodology

A metric without governance is a guess. Mattei's METRICS acronym unpacked: Mission, Executive, Trace, Relevant, Insightful, Computable, Security/Specificity. The per-metric runbook card template every metric must satisfy before it ships to a dashboard.

Lesson 0003 data-driven-cybersecurity 2026-07-19

The Audit-Ready Metrics Pack

The single page that survives every regulator's question. A 5-framework cross-reference matrix mapping every metric to UBCK Circular 17/2024, ISO 27001:2022 Annex A, PDPD Decree 13/2023, NIST CSF 2.0, and CIS Controls v8.

The two CSF functions the regulator asks first. Mattei ch. 3 (identify) + ch. 4 (govern). Strategic-foundation metrics: governance health, risk-baseline, asset-discovery coverage, third-party risk.

The statistical methods behind every ATLAS threshold. Mattei ch. 11: correlation, moving averages (SMA/EMA), Bayesian inference, Poisson distribution. The window-selection decision tree that turns "Mean + 2×SD" from a guess into a reproducible, auditable choice.
