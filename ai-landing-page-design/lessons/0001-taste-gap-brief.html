Taste Gap & Creative Director Brief

AI handles design rules — spacing, typography, hierarchy, colour theory. It does not handle taste — originality, meaning, feel. Your job, as the creative director of your own landing page, is to specify the things AI cannot decide. That is what this brief is.

Source: Leon Lin's "How To Actually Design With AI" X thread (per your summary; original tweet not retrieved at design time).
