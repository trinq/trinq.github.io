AI Landing Page Design

Six lessons that turn Leon Lin's design-with-AI framework into a real, shipped landing page you own. Each lesson is a self-contained HTML exercise with localStorage gating; finish them in order.

Central Insight. AI executes design rules well, but lacks taste. Each lesson is a workaround for that limitation — pick the narrowest workflow that still ships distinct work.

Lessons

Lesson 0001 ai-landing-page-design 2026-07-14

Taste Gap & Brief

Write a six-field creative-director brief (idea, audience, promise, feeling, anti-feeling, success signal). A vague-word guard blocks "clean"/"modern"/"professional" before save. ~6 minutes.

Lesson 0002 ai-landing-page-design 2026-07-14

Three Methods & Baseline

Pick the fastest method (Design Skill) or the balanced method (Inspiration Board) for a v0 baseline, then audit it against eight common slop patterns. ~7 minutes.

Lesson 0003 ai-landing-page-design 2026-07-14

Inspiration Board

Collect 12–20 references and annotate each with a verb-phrase principle, an anti-pattern, and a confidence rating. Requires 8+ sources and unique principles. ~9 minutes.

Lesson 0004 ai-landing-page-design 2026-07-14

Component-by-Component

Build Hero plus one other section, each with an intent paragraph, 2+ references, a 40-char minimum HTML payload, and a 360/768/1280 responsive pass. ~12 minutes.

Lesson 0005 ai-landing-page-design 2026-07-14

Assets & Motion

Specify one custom asset, layer it over a background, and add one interaction whose motion grammar is one of five verbs (settle, reveal, acknowledge, hold, release). ~10 minutes.

Lesson 0006 ai-landing-page-design 2026-07-14

Critique & Ship

Critique every section against the brief, mark each ship/iterate/cut, and require a 5/6 brief-alignment score plus two reflection fields. ~8 minutes.
