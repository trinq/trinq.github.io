Vietnam's Personal Data Protection Decree 356/2025 (effective 01/01/2026) and the 2025 Cybersecurity Law (effective 01/07/2026) reshape compliance for every enterprise operating in Vietnam. This course turns those two laws into a 6-step action plan for Pre Sales Security Consultants and in-house compliance teams: data mapping, internal records, DPO appointment, DPIA/CTIA, vendor contracts, and staff training.
