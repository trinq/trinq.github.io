Design with AI

A teaching framework for human-AI collaborative design. AI executes design rules well, but lacks taste. Every method here is a workaround for that limitation.

Central Insight. AI executes design rules well, but lacks taste. Every method is a workaround for this limitation.

Lessons

Lesson 0001 design-with-ai 2026-07-14

The Taste Gap — Why AI can't replace your creativity

The single most important idea in the framework: the difference between what AI can do for you and what you must do yourself. Reading time: ~5 minutes.

Lesson 0002 design-with-ai 2026-07-14

Design Skills — The fast approach

The fastest method. Use prebuilt design skills (Impeccable, TasteSkill, Emil Kowalski's UI skills) when short on time. Reading time: ~5 minutes.

Lesson 0003 design-with-ai 2026-07-14

Inspiration Board — The balanced approach

Curate reference images, then direct AI to combine styles. Faster than building piece by piece, more distinctive than using a prebuilt skill. Reading time: ~7 minutes.

Lesson 0004 design-with-ai 2026-07-14

Component-by-Component — Highest quality

Build piece by piece: define meaning, collect references, build each component. The most work, the highest quality. Reading time: ~10 minutes.

Lesson 0005 design-with-ai 2026-07-14

Reference Collection — Building your library

Every screenshot you save and annotate builds the muscle AI can't replicate. The daily habit that compounds. Reading time: ~6 minutes.

Lesson 0006 design-with-ai 2026-07-14

Prompt Crafting — Translating taste into instructions

A great reference library doesn't help if you can't translate it into instructions. The prompt patterns that turn your taste into AI output. Reading time: ~8 minutes.

Lesson 0007 design-with-ai 2026-07-14

Asset Generation — Custom imagery, icons, video

The fastest way to differentiate a design is custom imagery. The workflow, the tools, and how to prompt them well. Reading time: ~8 minutes.

Lesson 0008 design-with-ai 2026-07-14

Interactions and Motion — Making design feel alive

Static design is half a design. The principles and timing that make interfaces feel alive: duration, easing, hover states, transitions, accessibility. Reading time: ~7 minutes.

Lesson 0009 design-with-ai 2026-07-14

Refinement and Critique — Knowing when to change what

The meta-skill that sits above all others. The critique framework, the four-level refinement ladder, the one-change rule, AI-default patterns to override. Reading time: ~8 minutes.

Lesson 0010 design-with-ai 2026-07-14

Shipping the Product — The last mile

A design that lives on a Figma canvas affects no one. The ship readiness checklist, good-enough decision matrix, five launch principles, post-launch observation. Reading time: ~6 minutes.
