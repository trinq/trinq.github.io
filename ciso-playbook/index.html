CISO Playbook

Eleven lessons built from CISO Desk Reference Guide Volume 2 (Bonney, Hayslip, Stamper, 2018), adapted for a Security Leader formalising into CISO at a 9-entity Vietnamese securities firm. Every lesson produces a Vietnamese artefact you can circulate to your CEO, board, audit committee, UBCK examiner, or team this week.

Arc 1 — Become the CISO the firm already needs

Lesson 0001 ciso-playbook 2026-07-19

The First 90 Days

The CISO interview showpiece. Days 0–30 build the assessment baseline; 30–60 build the team + governance; 60–90 build the first deliverables. Vietnamese artefact: a one-page 90-day plan you hand to the CEO this week.

Lesson 0002 ciso-playbook 2026-07-19

The Blended-Capability Team

The team is the constraint on every other decision. Build / buy / borrow / bot quadrants, Bonney's three questions for every role, Hayslip's skills matrix adapted across 9 entities. Vietnamese artefact: operating model.

Lesson 0003 ciso-playbook 2026-07-19

Awareness That Changes Behaviour

The cheapest, most regulator-friendly, most visible intervention you can make this quarter — and the easiest to get wrong. CIS Five Verbs, the trusted-agent layer. Vietnamese artefact: a one-pager the entire firm reads in 5 minutes.

Lesson 0004 ciso-playbook 2026-07-19

Monitoring Without Lying

The board's dread question: "are we secure?" The reasonable-care doctrine, Stamper's two-form split (board form + ops form), Bonney's 13-item watch list. Vietnamese artefact: one-pager the board reads in 90 seconds.

Arc 2 — Run the programme when something goes wrong

Lesson 0005 ciso-playbook 2026-07-19

Threat Intel as a Program

Turn the camera outward. Stamper's Six Keys, Bonney's human network as structural argument, Hayslip's OSINT starter kit, the polymorphic-malware / 24-hour phishing-site / RaaS reality. Vietnamese artefact: programme outline.

Lesson 0006 ciso-playbook 2026-07-19

Continuity, Backups, RTO/RPO

The artefacts that determine whether a disruption lasts 30 minutes or 30 days. The BIA matrix, FIPS 199 tiers, Bonney's defend-the-backups move. Vietnamese artefact: BIA matrix per process.

Lesson 0007 ciso-playbook 2026-07-19

Incident Response, The Playbook

What you do in the moment when something triggers. Bonney's virtuous cycle, Stamper's 5 critical assumptions + 'document the obvious', the RACI matrix, 5 runbooks, the quarterly test cadence. Vietnamese artefact: the playbook.

Lesson 0008 ciso-playbook 2026-07-19

Recovery, Resuming Operations

Recover the process, not the asset. The 5-stage recovery sequence, the persistence problem (Bonney's most important warning), Stamper's 7 swim-lanes, Hayslip's DOJ 8-step readiness scorecard. Vietnamese artefact: recovery playbook.

Lesson 0009 ciso-playbook 2026-07-19

Forensics & Post-Mortem

Two purposes (legal + prevention), the Cyber Kill Chain, Stamper's 5 forensic stages + Heisenberg principle + attribution as a four-letter word. Vietnamese artefact: forensics + post-mortem playbook with chain-of-custody template.

Arc 3 — Organise, then outlive yourself

Lesson 0010 ciso-playbook 2026-07-19

Strategic Plan, The Day Job

The 1-page-per-objective + initiatives + owners + dates document that operates as the day-to-day manual. SWOT, Moore's core-vs-context, build-from-scratch playbook, MSSP due diligence. Vietnamese artefact: the 5–15-page strategic plan.

Lesson 0011 ciso-playbook 2026-07-19

Handover, The Final Lesson

Stewardship, not ownership. The 8-component handover package built continuously, updated quarterly, audited annually. The discipline that makes the programme survive the CISO's departure. Vietnamese artefact: handover package.
