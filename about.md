<p>I'm a Pre Sales Security Consultant at <a href="http://www.cmcsisg.vn/">CMC SI Saigon</a>, based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. I spend my days helping organizations understand their security needs and find the right solutions before a sale is made — part architect, part translator between business risk and technical controls.</p>

<h3>What I do</h3>

<p>My work sits at the intersection of security strategy and sales engineering. I build proof-of-concepts, run threat modeling workshops, respond to RFPs, and help customers move from "we need security" to "here's how we reduce our risk." The work requires keeping up with the threat landscape, understanding a broad range of security products, and explaining complex things clearly.</p>

<h3>Beyond work</h3>

<p>When I'm not working, I read nonfiction — mostly security, psychology, and systems thinking. I'm building this site partly as a way to think out loud about what I'm learning. I also play games, travel when I can, and drink a lot of coffee.</p>
