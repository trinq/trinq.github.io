<h3>The four prompts</h3>

<ol>
  <li><strong>Skeleton before the flesh</strong> — What is the central argument? What are the 5 load-bearing ideas? Run this before opening page one.</li>
  <li><strong>Find the load-bearing chapters</strong> — Which 4–6 chapters out of 10–14 actually matter? Skip the rest.</li>
  <li><strong>Stress-test the book</strong> — What would a hostile critic say? Where is the evidence weakest? Steelman with citations.</li>
  <li><strong>Compress until you understand</strong> — Explain it to a 14-year-old in 3 sentences. What is the one actionable thing?</li>
</ol>
