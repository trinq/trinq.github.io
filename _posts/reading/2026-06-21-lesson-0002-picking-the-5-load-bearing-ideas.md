<h3>The bottleneck</h3>

<p>Three of the four prompts can be run in five minutes each. The "5 ideas" step takes 30–60 minutes if you do it well. That is not a bug — the effort of deciding what matters is the learning event.</p>

<h3>The 3-part test</h3>

<p>An idea is load-bearing if it passes at least two of three tests:</p>

<ol>
  <li><strong>The exception test</strong> — Would removing this idea break the book's central argument?</li>
  <li><strong>The conversation test</strong> — Would you reference this idea in a debate about the book's topic?</li>
  <li><strong>The six-month test</strong> — Will you still care about this idea after six months?</li>
</ol>
